# Backup & Restore

## Overview

Backup & Restore is the simplest and lowest-cost DR strategy.

In this approach, backups are stored safely and infrastructure is rebuilt or restored when disaster happens.

## Typical Use Case

Suitable for:

- non-critical applications
- internal tools
- systems where recovery can take longer
- workloads with limited DR budget

## Expected Recovery Profile

- RTO: High
- RPO: Medium to High
